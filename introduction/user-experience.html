User experience

  • Expansion in the language of sexuality recognises the diversity of human experience
  • However, orientation to the binary still permeates the way non-binary people experience the world
  • On OkCupid:
    • select up to 5 of 22 gender categories, including "intersex and gender-adjacent categories like transgender, transfeminine and transmasculine" (Jas 2020: 72)
    • "the moment after you pick anything other than either woman or man and select 'Done', the joy of recognition is quickly cut short by a sudden slap of 'administrative violence' (Spade 2015)" (Jas 2020: 72)
    • as seen in the left image below, "you are asked whether you wish to be included in searches for women or men: 'pick one'" (Jas 2020: 72)
