OkCupid

  • New sexuality options introduced in 2014 (Buchanan 2014) cover:
    • "different levels or lack of sexual attraction (asexual)
    • different motivations or requirements for sexual desire (demisexual; sapiosexual)
    • fluidity (hetero/homoflexible)
    • more than two genders (pansexual)
    • uncertainty (questioning)
    • ableism (sapiosexual; see e.g. Blum 2016; Moss 2015)" (Jas 2020: 71, with minor stylistic edits)
