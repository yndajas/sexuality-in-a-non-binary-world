  • The asexual community has been a source of conceptual and linguistic innovation:
    • 'Sexual' versus 'asexual' ('sexual' is sometimes called 'allosexual', but with 'allo' meaning other, it doesn't make complete sense and 'sexual' seems to be preferred)
    • Split attraction model: romantic versus sexual attraction
  • Focus here is on sexual attraction (and arousal and desire) among sexual people
